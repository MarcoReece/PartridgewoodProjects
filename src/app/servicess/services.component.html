Painting Services

Painting adds contrast and cleanliness to a structure, enhancing its appearance and protecting surfaces. Our painting service involves a systematic approach to ensure a lasting and beautiful finish. The steps of our painting process include:

  1. Surface Preparation

    Your home will be pressure washed to remove loose paint, dust and debris. All plaster is checked for cracks and delamination to create a stable surface. All wood work and metal work is sand-prepared to create a surface that is ready for the final product.

  2. Primer Application

    Trimwork, all eaves, gutters, down-pipes, facia boards, window frames, doors and door frames are prepared and painted using the appropriate primer, if nessesary, followed by a two coat application best suited for the specific surface to be treated.

  3. Painting

    Once the surfaces have been properly prepared, the appropriate coating is applied to suitedinterior or exterior, metal work or wood.

  4. Cleanup

    After the painting is completed, we meticulously clean up the work area. This includes removing paint splatters, packing up equipment, and ensuring your property is left in a clean and tidy condition.

  5. Drying Time

    We allow sufficient time for the paint to dry, ensuring that it cures properly. The drying time varies depending on the type of paint used and environmental conditions.

  6. Inspect and Touch Up

    After the paint has dried, our team conducts a thorough inspection of the painted surfaces. Any imperfections or areas that require touch-up are identified and addressed to achieve a flawless result.

Damp and Water Proofing Services

Waterproofing is essential to protect your structure from moisture damage and to ensure its longevity.

  1. Plaster Removal

    With rising damp, the plaster is chopped off the wall, to a height of one metre to expose the bare brick underneath.

  2. Chemical Injection

    A damp proof chemical is injected at the damp proof course level to saturate bricks under pressure to create a new D.P.C (Damp proof course).

  3. Slurry and Replastering

    The bare bricks will then be coated with a tanking slurry to avoid future lateral damp. The area will the be replastered with 42.5 surebuild cement, matching the existing plaster.

Tiling Services

Tiling adds aesthetic appeal and functionality to various areas of your property. The tiling process consists of these steps:

  1. Surface Preparation

    We prepare the surface by ensuring it is clean, level, and free from any imperfections.

  2. Tile Layout

    Our team carefully plans the tile layout, ensuring a visually pleasing and symmetrical design.

  3. Adhesive and Tile Installation

    Tiles are securely affixed using the appropriate adhesive, with precise spacing and alignment.

  4. Grout Application

    Grout is applied to fill the gaps between tiles, providing stability and a finished look.

  5. Sealing and Cleaning

    After the grout has cured, tiles are sealed for protection, and the entire surface is thoroughly cleaned.

  6. Final Inspection

    We conduct a final inspection to ensure all tiles are correctly installed and that the finish is flawless.
